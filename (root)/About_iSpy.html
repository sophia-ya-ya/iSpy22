About the Glasses

The glasses are constructed from carbon fibre, a material chosen for its non-conductive properties, ensuring that they do not generate electricity. This enhances the overall experience for our customers. Additionally, the design features a hole for attaching a neck strap, which provides extra security and reduces the likelihood of theft. This is particularly important for our visually impaired customers, as it helps prevent them from inadvertently losing their glasses.
